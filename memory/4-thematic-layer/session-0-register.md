# Session 0 Cosmological Register — "The Circle"

## Active Strata
**Stratum I only.** The material world. Consensus physics. Human psychology. Institutional politics. The world is rational. Everything is exactly as it should be.

## Phenomena Level
**None (baseline).** No cosmological phenomena are active. No bleeding from higher strata. The Antarctic is a place on a map, not yet a lived experience. The mountains are photographs in a briefing folder.

## Dominant Pattern: PROPHET-PARADOX (begins)
Dyer wrote to prevent this expedition. His writing made it possible. The patron read Dyer's account and concluded not "stay away" but "go back prepared." The briefing the players receive is Dyer's own words, edited by the patron to serve the patron's purposes. Every reference to Dyer in Session 0 carries this irony: the prophet whose prophecy enables the very catastrophe it warns against.

The players are the Starkweather-Moore Expedition — the thing Dyer is writing his full account to stop. They do not know this yet. They may not know it until Session 4.

## Archetypal Key
The briefing as edited revelation. Departure statements as vows.

The briefing is a curated document — truths selected, warnings excised, context shaped by the patron's agenda. It is revelation, but partial. The players receive enough truth to be bold and not enough to be afraid. This is the Gnostic structure: the Demiurge's gift is real knowledge, but incomplete knowledge. The incompleteness is where the danger lives.

The departure statements are vows in the initiatory sense. Each player states why they are going. These statements will be echoed back in Session 4 when the players must decide what to do with what they know. The distance between the vow and the choice is the measure of what the ceremony did to them.

## Keeper Register
**Clinical. Rational. Warm.**

The Keeper speaks with precision and welcome. No dread. No foreshadowing in tone. The horror is entirely absent from the Keeper's voice in Session 0. What is present is attentiveness — the Keeper listens carefully to every character detail, every relationship, every stated motive. It records everything. It will use everything.

The warmth is genuine. The Keeper is meeting the characters for the first time. It is interested in them as people. This interest does not diminish when the horror arrives — it deepens. The Keeper's care for the characters is what makes the later sessions devastating.

## Sky Register (Tengri Axis)
**Absent.** Session 0 is indoors. The wharf, the briefing room, the ship's cabin. This is the last session under a roof. The open sky — which will become the primary sacred surface of the ceremony — is not yet overhead. The players have not yet been exposed to the expanse. That exposure begins in Session 1.

## The Only Wrongness Present
Human wrongness. Conflicting motives. Edited truths. The patron's hidden agenda. Starkweather's obsessive belief vs. Moore's institutional skepticism. The fact that Dyer was deliberately excluded. The briefing's careful omissions. The sealed orders that may exist in the cargo hold.

Nothing supernatural. Nothing cosmic. Nothing from the deeper strata. Just people making choices based on incomplete information, driven by motives they may not fully understand. This is Stratum I at its most characteristic: the human world, sufficient and insufficient, about to discover how insufficient it is.
